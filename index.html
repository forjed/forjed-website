Resulti

Resulti is the first service by Forjed. It a privacy-focused and safesearch search engine founded the December 30th 2022. Resulti is the most popular service by Forjed.

Part of the Resulti brands, ProxyProxy is a proxy to escape censorship and navigate anonymously. It can be used at school to use blocked website

Clickette is a search engine created by ClaytonTDM, a developer from Mississauga in Canada. Clickette shut down, but Forjed and ClaytonTDM aggressed to make Clickette as a part of Forjed.

Weboo
